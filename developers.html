Koki

Lead Robotics Engineer & AI Specialist

Koki earned both his M.Eng and Ph.D. from the University of Tokyo's Department of Mechano-Informatics, where his dissertation focused on compliant manipulation for service robots. Raised in Sapporo in a family of calligraphers, he merged artistic heritage with high-precision robotics, publishing a dozen papers on visual servoing before founding the goghai initiative.
